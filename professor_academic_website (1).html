Who I Am

About Me

With over two decades of clinical practice, academic mentorship, and surgical research, I am committed to building the next generation of surgeons who combine scientific rigor with compassionate patient care. My work spans general surgery, urology, and pediatric surgery — from the operating theatre to the lecture hall.

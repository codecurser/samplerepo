Who I Am

About Me

I am Dr. Mohit Kumar Mathur, Professor of General Surgery at GIMS, Greater Noida. I am heading a surgery unit and dealing both Adult and pediatric surgical patients. My journey in medicine began at SMS Medical College, Jaipur, and took me through rigorous training across India's most prestigious institutions.

Surgery and teaching are more than professions to me—they are callings I pursue with heart and purpose. I believe in creating future surgeons who are not only skilled but also empathetic and innovative in their approach to patient care.

"Dedicated to pushing boundaries and nurturing talent, I'm committed to holistic healing—where expertise meets compassion."
